  • Goals and working perspectives

    The Faculty of Law of the French University in Armenia prepares future lawyers in the fields of business, judicial, administrative and international law.

    Decisively directed towards the professional world, specializations of the Faculty of Law enable future specialists to quickly start their practical work:

    In the field of international business, being able to solve and manage legal problems related to various aspects (taxation, financial, accounting, etc.) of company management

    In the field of judicial and administrative law, being able to specialize further as attorneys, bailiffs.

  • UFAR Faculty of Law Graduates can work as:

    Barristers

    Attorneys

    Legal advisers

    Assistants to the judge

    Diplomats

    Legal issue experts

    Notaries

    Prosecutors, etc.

  • Diplomas

    The Faculty of Law offers two Degrees of education –Licence.

    For Masters

    International, International Private and Comparative Law Master /corresponding Master

  • admission

    Purpose
    The aim of undergraduate academic programmes (hereinafter referred to as AP) is to provide basic knowledge and specialization in the field of vocational education that will allow the graduates to succeed in middle-income positions.

    Duration
    The duration of the study is 4 years.

    Deadline for submitting documents:
    From May 15 to June 15, 2019, from Monday to Friday, from 10:00 to 16:00.

    It is necessary to submit the following documents:

    Application form

    A copy of a secondary education certificate or a document, certifying the fact that the applicant is a 12-grade pupil

    Two photos 3x4 size
    Military record sheet

    Receipt for payment for the admission of the document (7200 AMD)

    Document verifying the selection of elective disciplines.
