The Student Council of the French University in Armenia is a representative self-governing body whose members are elected and appointed from the UFAR students. According to the RA legislation, the powers of the Student Council and the procedure for their activities are defined by the UFAR Charter and the decisions of the UFAR governing bodies. The Student Council represents and defends students' interests, promotes their academic, creative, moral and physical development, cooperates with other higher educational institutions and educational organizations in Armenia and abroad.
