  • admission regulation

    The duration of the course is 39 weeks (from September 09, 2021 to June 7, 2022)

    Teaching is provided by experienced instructors in small groups, from Monday to Friday from 15:30 to 18:20
    The experience of previous years shows that about 80% of the students involved in Preparatory Courses are successfully admitted to the University to pursue their undergraduate studies.

  • Tuition Fee

    The annual fee for preparatory courses are the following

    Armenian | 110.000 AMD

    Foreign Language (French, English, German) | 120.000 AMD

    Social Sciences | 120.000 AMD

    Mathematics (for the faculties of Management, Marketing, and Finance) | 120.000 AMD

    Mathematics (for the Faculty of IT and Applied Mathematics) | 150.000 AMD

    The first half of the sum is to be paid until September 20, 2021 and the other half until January 17, 2022.

  • application

    Applications are accepted from July 08 to 25 and from August 26 to 30. Applicants can enroll in-between the academic year, having an opportunity to attend the rest of the course, paying the appropriate amount

    To enroll, the following documents must be submitted to room 201

    Completed application form;

    Copy of pages 2, 3 and 5 of the passport or the copy of the birth certificate (the authentic copy is necessary while documents are submitted);

    Completed and signed contract (to be filled in on the spot, for underage applicants the presence of one of the parents, and the parent’s passport is obligatory);

    1 picture of 3x4 size,
    A receipt for the amount paid in the accounting, room 102 (6,000 AMD)

  • Contest registration test 11

    The 2020-2021 registration will be held on site at UFAR and online from May 17 to June 11, 2021 inclusive , from Monday to Friday from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. May 28 is a non-working day.

    The complete file must contain the following documents:

    The completed form
    Photocopy of the certificate of completion of secondary studies (if available) or certificate from the school
    attesting that the candidate is studying in the 12th year
    Photocopy of passport or identity card. The original must be presented when submitting the application
    2 photos of the candidate, format 3 x 4 cm
    The certificate issued by the Military Commissariat
    Photocopy of the DELF certificate
    Payment receipt for entering the competition (7,500 drams for a simple application)
