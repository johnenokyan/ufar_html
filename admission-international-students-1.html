5 reasons to live & study in armenia

1. Affordable Living and Studying

Armenia is a great study destination as the costs of education is reasonable and the living expenses are relatively lower than those of other European countries. Also, the communication expenses are low. It is one of the best countries when to comes to affordability. Students can easily get accommodation in Armenia.

2. Welcoming, Friendly and Safe Environment

Armenian people are friendly and convivial. The country is safe to live. Public transportation is easily available and efficient.

3. Quality Education

Armenia is well-known country for higher education and research. It has some of the best universities. At the capital of the country are located many well-known higher education institutions. The institutions emphasize on studies in business, medicine and law. Armenia is known for its medical studies and many students from around the world flock to the country to be a part of this study programme.

4. Multi-cultural Experience

Armenia has diverse cultures. It is a blend of different cultures, values and religions. Studying in Armenia is a new experience. Different perspectives are valued and learning together is encouraged.

5. Full of Opportunities: Enhances your Career

Armenia offers a great opportunity for individuals who wish to broaden their experience and acquire skills that they can take forward into a successful career and life after the completion of their studies. The country also welcomes students who wish to return to Armenia and provide opportunity to start their own business.
