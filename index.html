UFAR, the French university in Armenia is a higher educational establis hment which was created by the governments of Armenia and France in 2000.

UFAR offers double Bachelor’s degrees in Law, Management, Marketing, Finance and Computer Science and Applied Mathematics, Master’s degrees in Law, Marketing and Management, and also doctoral studies in the fields of Management and Law. UFAR’s academic partners are University Lyon 3 and University Toulouse 3.
